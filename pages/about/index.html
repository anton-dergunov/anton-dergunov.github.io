    About

    Hi! My name is Anton Dergunov.

    I am excited about machine learning, data analysis and software engineering in general. At first I was fascinated with machine learning when studying at university. I was giving lectures about AI for several years. I applied machine learning techniques at work (including work at Yandex for ranking in video search). And I like playing with machine learning and data analysis in my spare time.

    I worked as a software engineer at Yandex, Intel, JP Morgan and Mera Networks, using C++, Java, Python, Bash and other programming languages and technologies. At Yandex I was doing feature engineering for ranking videos in Yandex Search engine. At Intel I was in a team implementing threading and performance profiler Intel VTune Amplifier, I implemented results of my PhD for automatic detection of known performance issues in MPI applications in Performance Assistant capability of Intel Trace Analyzer and Collector and later I was doing research performance analysis and optimization of parallel software.

    You may access my CV and my LinkedIn account.

    In my spare time, if I am not working on my side projects or studying something new about machine learning or new tech, I enjoy all kinds of sport (yoga, swimming, cycling, gym, skiing, etc.), I am travelling, cracking escape rooms, occasionally dancing tango and cooking tasty food.
