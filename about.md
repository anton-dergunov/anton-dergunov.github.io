Hi! My name is Anton Dergunov.

I am excited about machine learning, data analysis and software engineering
in general. At the moment I work as a Senior Applied Scientist at Microsoft
on machine learning projects (for example, new [Meeting Insights][meeting insights]
feature in Outlook).

Before Microsoft, I was working at [Yandex][yandex], Intel and some
other companies. At Yandex I was doing feature engineering for ranking videos in
[Yandex Search][yandex video] engine. At Intel I was in a team implementing
threading and performance profiler [Intel VTune Amplifier][vtune]. Later at Intel
I was doing research in performance analysis and optimization of parallel software.
I implemented results of my PhD for automatic detection of known performance issues in MPI
applications in [Performance Assistant][performance assistant] capability of [Intel Trace
Analyzer and Collector][itac].  My journey with machine learning
started at university where I was giving lectures about AI for several years.

You may access [my CV][cv] and [my LinkedIn account][linkedin].

I usually spend a lot of my spare time playing with machine learning and
data analysis, or learning new tech. Otherwise I enjoy all kinds of sport
(yoga, swimming, cycling, gym, skiing, etc.), travelling,
cracking escape rooms, cooking tasty food, etc.
